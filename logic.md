Activation Concept: Choose Your Digital Superpower
Core Idea
Visitors take a short digital quiz and discover their personal “digital superpower.” Based on their answers, they receive one of four digital profiles:
• The Connector
• The Explorer
• The Creator
• The Problem Solver
The quiz should be quick, fun, easy to answer, and completed in under 1 minute. After receiving their result, visitors receive a small profile-based branded item.
 
Digital Profiles
1. The Connector
Your digital superpower is connection.
You use technology to stay close to people, share moments, build relationships, and keep conversations going. For you, digital life is about people first.
2. The Explorer
Your digital superpower is curiosity.
You use technology to discover new ideas, learn new things, try new tools, and explore what is possible. You are always one step away from finding something interesting.
3. The Creator
Your digital superpower is imagination.
You use technology to create, design, post, write, edit, film, or express yourself. For you, the digital world is a place to turn ideas into something real.
4. The Problem Solver
Your digital superpower is efficiency.
You use technology to make life easier, faster, and more organized. You like tools that save time, simplify tasks, and help you get things done.
 
Quiz Questions
Each question should have four answer options. Each answer corresponds to one profile.
 
Question 1
What technology do you use the most?
A. Staying in touch with people
B. Discovering new things
C. Creating or sharing content
D. Making everyday tasks easier
Profile logic:
A = Connector
B = Explorer
C = Creator
D = Problem Solver
 
Question 2
What is the first thing you do when you open your phone?
A. Check messages or social media
B. Read, search, browse, or watch something new
C. Take a photo, post, edit, or create something
D. Check calendar, maps, banking, delivery, or useful apps
Profile logic:
A = Connector
B = Explorer
C = Creator
D = Problem Solver
 
Question 3
Which digital tool would you miss the most for one day?
A. Messaging and video calls
B. Search, maps, YouTube, or learning platforms
C. Camera, editing apps, music, or design tools
D. Banking, transport, delivery, calendar, or productivity apps
Profile logic:
A = Connector
B = Explorer
C = Creator
D = Problem Solver
 
Question 4
If technology gave you one superpower, what would you choose?
A. To connect instantly with anyone, anywhere
B. To discover answers and ideas faster
C. To turn any idea into content
D. To solve daily problems with one tap
Profile logic:
A = Connector
B = Explorer
C = Creator
D = Problem Solver
 
Result Logic
The profile with the highest number of selected answers becomes the visitor’s digital superpower.
Example:
• Mostly A answers = The Connector
• Mostly B answers = The Explorer
• Mostly C answers = The Creator
• Mostly D answers = The Problem Solver
If there is a tie, the system can either:
• use the answer to Question 4 as the deciding answer; or
• randomly select one of the tied profiles.
Preferred option: use Question 4 as the tiebreaker, because it is the most “superpower” oriented question.
 
Suggested Process Flow
Step 1: Attraction
A host invites visitors to participate:
The stand should clearly display the main activation message:
Choose Your Digital Superpower
Take the quiz. Discover your profile. Receive a souvenir.
 
Step 2: Registration and Consent
Visitor scans a QR code or uses a tablet at the stand.
They fill in a short registration form:
• Name
• Email
• Consent for data processing
• Consent to participate in the prize draw (if we decide to have a draw)
 
Step 3: Quiz
Visitor answers 4 short questions.
Each question has four answer options, connected to the four digital profiles.
The quiz should be visually simple and fast, with no long text.
 
Step 4: Result
The system shows the visitor’s digital profile:
Your Digital Superpower is: The Connector
or
Your Digital Superpower is: The Explorer
or
Your Digital Superpower is: The Creator
or
Your Digital Superpower is: The Problem Solver
The result screen should include:
• profile name;
• one short description;
• branded visual;
• confirmation that the visitor has joined the prize draw.
 
Step 5: Merch / Badge
The visitor receives a small item connected to their profile.
